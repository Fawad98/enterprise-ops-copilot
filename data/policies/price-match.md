# Zava Price Match Policy

**Policy ID:** POL-PM-001
**Effective date:** 2026-01-01
**Owner:** Merchandising

## 1. What we match

Zava matches the current advertised price of an identical, in-stock item from a qualifying competitor. The item must be the same brand, model, and configuration.

## 2. Qualifying competitors

Qualifying competitors are major national retailers with a public online price. Marketplace third-party sellers, auction sites, membership-only warehouse clubs, and clearance or liquidation listings do **not** qualify.

## 3. Time limits

- **Pre-purchase:** Request a price match before completing your order.
- **Post-purchase:** If Zava's own price drops within **14 days** of your purchase, request a one-time adjustment for the difference. Competitor price drops after purchase do not qualify — only Zava's own price drops qualify post-purchase.

## 4. Exclusions

The following are excluded from price matching:

- Doorbuster, Black Friday, and Cyber Monday pricing
- Bundle or gift-with-purchase offers
- Open-box, refurbished, or used items
- Pricing errors
- Items already discounted with an employee discount (see employee-discount.md)

## 5. How the adjustment is applied

An approved price match is applied as a refund of the difference to the original payment method. Price matches cannot be combined with the employee discount or promotional codes.
