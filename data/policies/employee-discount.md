# Zava Employee Discount Policy

**Policy ID:** POL-EMP-001
**Effective date:** 2026-01-01
**Owner:** Human Resources

## 1. Eligibility

All active Zava employees who have completed their first 30 days of employment are eligible for the employee discount. Contractors and seasonal staff are eligible only if their contract explicitly states so.

## 2. Discount amount

| Category | Employee discount |
|---|---|
| Apparel | 30% |
| Home | 25% |
| Toys | 25% |
| Electronics | 10% |
| Grocery | 5% |

The employee discount cannot be combined with promotional discounts or price-match adjustments. Where multiple discounts could apply, the customer receives the single largest discount, not the sum.

## 3. Purchase limits

Employee-discounted purchases are limited to **$2,000 in discounted value per calendar year**. Purchases beyond this limit are charged at standard price.

## 4. Prohibited use

The employee discount is for personal and immediate-household use only. Reselling employee-discounted merchandise is grounds for termination. Employees may not use their discount to purchase on behalf of non-household members.

## 5. Returns on discounted purchases

Employee-discounted purchases follow the standard returns policy (returns-policy.md), but refunds are issued at the discounted price paid, not the standard price.
