# Zava Shipping Policy

**Policy ID:** POL-SHP-001
**Effective date:** 2026-01-01
**Owner:** Fulfillment

## 1. Shipping methods and SLAs

| Method | Delivery SLA (business days) | Cost |
|---|---|---|
| Standard | 5–7 | Free on orders over $35; otherwise $4.99 |
| Expedited | 2–3 | $9.99 |
| Overnight | 1 | $24.99 |

SLA counts business days after the order ships, not after it is placed. Orders placed after 2:00 PM local warehouse time ship the next business day.

## 2. Carriers

Zava ships via three carriers: GroundLink (standard), SwiftPost (expedited and overnight), and RegionalCourier (select metro areas only). Customers cannot choose their carrier; the carrier is selected automatically based on destination and method.

## 3. Free shipping threshold

Orders with a merchandise subtotal of **$35 or more** qualify for free Standard shipping. The threshold is calculated before tax and after any discounts are applied. Gift cards do not count toward the threshold.

## 4. International shipping

Zava ships to Canada and Mexico only. International orders:

- Ship via SwiftPost International with a 7–12 business day SLA.
- Are subject to destination customs duties and taxes paid by the customer.
- Cannot use the free shipping threshold; international shipping starts at $19.99.

## 5. Order tracking

A tracking number is emailed when the order ships. Tracking updates may lag the carrier by up to 24 hours.

## 6. Undeliverable and lost packages

A package is considered **lost** if tracking shows no movement for 10 business days past the SLA, or if the carrier confirms loss. Lost packages are handled as a fulfillment failure: the customer receives a free replacement or refund, and Zava files a carrier claim. Do not charge the customer for reshipment of a lost package.
