# Zava Returns Policy

**Policy ID:** POL-RET-001
**Effective date:** 2026-01-01
**Owner:** Customer Operations

## 1. Standard return window

Customers may return most items within **30 days** of the delivery date for a full refund, provided the item is in resellable condition. The delivery date is the date recorded by the carrier, not the order date.

## 2. Return windows by category

| Category | Return window | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Apparel | 45 days | Must have original tags attached |
| Home | 30 days | — |
| Toys | 30 days | — |
| Grocery | 7 days | Perishable items are non-returnable (see Section 5) |
| Electronics | 30 days | See Section 3 for opened-item rules |

Where a category window in this table differs from the standard 30-day window in Section 1, **the category window takes precedence**.

## 3. Opened electronics

Opened electronics may be returned within the 30-day Electronics window, but a **15% restocking fee** applies to any opened electronic item. Unopened electronics returned within the window incur no restocking fee.

Items that cannot be returned once opened for hygiene or safety reasons (headphones, earbuds, personal grooming devices) are covered separately in Section 5.

## 4. Refund method and timing

Refunds are issued to the original payment method. Once a return is received and inspected, refunds are processed within **5 business days**. Customers who paid by store credit are refunded to store credit.

## 5. Non-returnable items

The following are non-returnable:

- Perishable grocery items
- Opened headphones, earbuds, and personal grooming devices (hygiene)
- Gift cards
- Digital downloads once accessed
- Custom or personalized orders

## 6. Damaged or defective items

If an item arrives damaged or defective, it is **not** subject to the restocking fee, and the customer is entitled to a free replacement or full refund regardless of category window. The handling procedure for damaged goods is defined in **damaged-goods-procedure.md**. Report damage within 14 days of delivery.

## 7. Return shipping

For standard returns, the customer pays return shipping. For damaged, defective, or incorrect items, Zava pays return shipping via a prepaid label.
